Breathing Light

Breathing light, which seems a strange thing to us, but in fact, we can see this kind of device almost everywhere. For instance, we can always see the breathing lights on our computers, especially laptops or PCs, at the back (cover) of the computers, there is always a light which keeps bright during use and shine gently as your computer is “sleeping”, that is the thing called “Breathing Light.”

During last week I tried to make a breathing light with my tutor and my team.

Obviously it doesn’t contains a rather complex steps, simply, I define the PIN on the Arduino as 9 hence the Arduino can send out the signals. The fade value determines the rate of the light to fade and relighted again.

When fadevalue is 0, the light will be lighted up and delay for 30 ms till the fade value equals to 255 or even larger.

If we code correctly, the breathing lights are extremely easy to make.
