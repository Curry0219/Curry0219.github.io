My Project in STEP 2

After a long period of time, my project has the whole change, you can see it in the picture below. Luckily, I was inspired by my first draft and analyzed its benefit and several drawbacks.

It is really simple to see that this structure is extremely unstable due to that the connection between the wheel, the engine and the main support is too simple and the width, thickness is too small. In fact, I wanted to add some process bar, but later I realized that almost all the connection is active and which cannot be fixed because I want this car can work just as the Curiosity, after the front wheel crosses the obstacle, the rear wheel is not affected, they can still roll horizontally until they meet the obstacle. All in all, the benefit of this structure is that there are three wheels in one side of the whole car, and while crossing the obstacle, it can make sure that there are always two wheels contact with the ground. That is the biggest benefit of this structure. So because of this reason, I persisted this structure and finally, for about few weeks later, I came up with the second draft, here we GOOO!

As you see, I paid about a month on this draft, I built a whole new assembly and several drawings of parts, I draw the model and the draft of each part off the whole structure and finally use the cooperation in the assembly to create this work.

Just like I mentioned before, the drawback of the structure before is too fragile and unstable, hence I thicken the thickness of the connection and the poles, which can increase the stability of the car without change the structure.

Another thing that I think is note-worthy is that I added the suspension system, which enables the body and the wheels of the carriage to move independently, which also greatly increase the notability and the ability of the crossing obstacles.

Well that’s all for the report for recent time, hope to see you next time!!!
