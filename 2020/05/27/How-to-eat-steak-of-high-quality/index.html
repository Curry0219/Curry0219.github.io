How to eat steak of high quality.

Some information about steaks

In dictionary, the definition of steak is a slice of meat , while in our lives, in western food, steaks are indispensible part, people cook them for meals, but it is unimaginable that there are such many variable methods to cook steak in western cookbooks. Moreover, as a high grade ingredients, steak always matched with some other food, the most particular one is wine, which concludes plenty of lore as well, for instance, Sirloin is the easiest one to go with wines, Cabernet Sauvignon, Zinfandel and Red Bordeaux all fit. And in the following context, I will introduce the classification, source and cooking style for you.

What’s the difference?

The way and the part we dissect decides different types of steaks. Cuts of steak can be broken down into three sections. Starting on the upper back and moving down to the mid-back you have the rib, the short loin, and the sirloin. The rib contains cuts such as the rib roast, the rib-eye steak, and the back ribs. This is the least tender section of the three. The short loin produces the T-bone, top loin steak, tenderloin, and the porterhouse. The sirloin gives the sirloin steak and the top sirloin. Other steaks like the chuck, round and flank steak come from those respective areas and tend to be tough cuts of meat. Strip steak, like the New York Steak, is cut from the T-bone portion.

The most tender cut of beef is the tenderloin. From this area, you will get cuts like chateaubriand, filet mignon, and tournedos. Though these cuts are tender, they are less flavorful. The rib-eye is less tender but far more flavorful. The same holds true about the sirloin cut.

In addition, the Fillet is the most tender part of a cow, and it barely contains fat, it is recommended to be cooked as medium rare. Sirloin, has fat at the top of the steak, it tastes more tough hence medium rare is best choice or we will feel tired to chew. Another one is Rib-eye, it is noticeable that they have fat in the medium part of the steak, and it should be cooked as medium. The last one is T-bone, it is the combination of Sirloin and Fillet, medium is recommended.

About tastes

The first one is blue rare, which is the most suitable one for the people who enjoys the taste of blood and the feeling of worry. This kind of steak is full of muscle hemoglobin, which is blood-liked. It is cooked by fry steak on both side for 30-60 seconds in order to maintain the dampness of the beef and create the difference of taste between the outside and inside of steak.

The next one is rare, it is similar to the blue-rare but it is cooked for longer time hence there are more parts are cooked.

Then, the medium rare!! The meat will conduct the heat to the center but doesn’t make too much change, when it is dissected, we can obtain that the upside and downside is cooked beef and the central part is barely cooked, this kind of steak is more juicy.

The medium is not rarely to see, it is the most well balanced one, the inside of the steak is pink and properly fried, which keeps balanced temperature and taste.

Medium well is eaten most in China, people prefer the cooked meet but not relatively rare. The medium well steak is chew resistance. So does the well done steak, which is totally cooked and owns the brown-red appearance.

Moreover, there’s particular care to the sauce that the steak used. There are several types of sauces, and the most common one is Peppercorn, it has alcohol in it and tastes salty. Another one called Steak Sauce, it is smoky flavour, mushroom sauce is another good choice to eat together with steak. It is made by mix beurre and alcohol and mushroom.
