Why I start to love Tesla

Yesterday was an amazing day, since I want to learn design engineering in the future, I decided to learn more about Tesla. Before yesterday, I have just heard about there is a fantastic company in this world called Tesla and its finder Elon Musk has a big dream which is bring human beings to all the universe, moreover, the cars from Tesla looks scientific and cool. In recent years, it is not rare to hear about some news about Tesla, SpaceX or Mr. Elon, they launch the rockets frequently and even launched a car. In addition, I started to notice this company more is the interview about Elon Musk and Buffett. In the interview they don’t praise each other but shows some hostile, and some people made the comment that Elon Musk is greater than Buffett in some ways. And heard of that I was totally shocked because in my opinion at that time, Buffett is a very rich businessman, but if someone is greater, this man must have done something impressive. After that, I begin to pay some attention on this company, because to me, it is not only a simple company for me anymore, it sounds cool. On yesterday, I did a very detailed research about Tesla and SpaceX. The biggest thing that Tesla impressed me is not its capital, its finance but its dream and the characteristics of this gorgeous enterprise.

About Tesla

Firstly I will show you some information from wikipedia

Tesla, Inc. (formerly Tesla Motors, Inc.) is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company based in Palo Alto, California. The company specializes in electric vehicle manufacturing, battery energy storage from home to grid scale and, through its acquisition of SolarCity, solar panel and solar roof tile manufacturing.

One note-worthy point s that the batteries Tesla uses is totally different from the other electric vehicles, it use thousands of small lithium-ion commodity cells instead of the large battery cells, which is cheaper to be manufactured and lighter as they are installed because they no longer need safety features due to the advanced thermal management system and an intumescent chemical in the battery can prevent fires.

This is only part of their advanced technology but I can’t understand very well recently, maybe I can work for them and design some fabulous systems or equipments.

That experience must be wonderful.
