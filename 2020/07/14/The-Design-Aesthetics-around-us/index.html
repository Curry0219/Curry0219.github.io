Design engineering

Posted on 2020-07-14

Design

Design is everywhere around us, which is also my goal course in the university, which develops as the time goes and the fashion changes, it deeply affect and changed our lives in several ways. Design is classified according to their purposes such as the industrial design, room design, architecture design and some others. In this article, I would like to share some essays and academic information about design engineering , which I really interested in.

Different from other designers, the design engineers tend to work on products and systems that involve adapting and using complex scientific and mathematical techniques. The emphasis tends to be on utilizing engineering physics and other applied sciences to develop solutions for society. Most of them prefer to work in a team of other engineers and other types of designers to develop or create a production from different dimensions.

Salary!

As a heated career, the salary of design engineer is also relatively high, which average is $68,127 and the data information is from PayScale. The top respondents for the job title Design Engineer are from the companies The Boeing Company, Intel Corporation and Caterpillar, Inc.. Reported salaries are highest at Intel Corporation where the average pay is $117,197. Other companies that offer high salaries for this role include The Boeing Company and Ford Motor Company, earning around $97,357 and $82,170, respectively. EN Engineering, Inc. pays the lowest at around $60,557. Caterpillar, Inc. and General Electric Co (GE) also pay on the lower end of the scale, paying $77,424 and $80,556, respectively. It is also noteworthy that the expertise and experience is extremely important in the area of design engineering, which we can see from the graph, the longer the time you work in the area of design engineering, the higher the payment you will get.

An entry-level Design Engineer with less than 1 year experience can expect to earn an average total compensation (includes tips, bonus, and overtime pay) of $60,845 based on 921 salaries. An early career Design Engineer with 1-4 years of experience earns an average total compensation of $65,198 based on 4,381 salaries. A mid-career Design Engineer with 5-9 years of experience earns an average total compensation of $73,891 based on 2,099 salaries. An experienced Design Engineer with 10-19 years of experience earns an average total compensation of $81,229 based on 1,087 salaries. In their late career (20 years and higher), employees earn an average total compensation of $85,699.

Career and responsibilities

The design engineersâ€™ career is to transform all the ideas into real products. Design engineers study, research and develop ideas for new products and the systems used to make them. They also modify existing products or processes to increase efficiency or improve performance. They work on almost every consumer product imaginable for large-scale production, from telephones and medical equipment to kitchen appliances and car engines.

Design engineers are not only concerned with making products that look good and are easy and safe to use: they are also concerned with ensuring that the product can be made cost-effectively and efficiently.
