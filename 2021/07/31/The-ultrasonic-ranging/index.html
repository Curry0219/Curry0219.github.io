The ultrasonic ranging

Posted on 2021-07-31

Lately I have been extremely busy, I need to carry on the preview of my CAIE A2 contents, and also keep on the production of The Progress, and the main part of the Progress has been re-designed, and I plan to show it to you in the next blog, because there are too much details to tell you about.

And in this blog, I plan to introduce the latest software which has already been installed on the Progress, which is the ultrasonic ranging part. In order to help complete its mission better in the fire, we need to know the probable range of the fire, which can help the firefighters decide that what kind of the techniques they should bring and what kind of the method they should take.

I had been had a conversation with a firefighter in China, and during that conversation, I noticed that their main mission when they entre the fire is to find out the point of the resource of the fire, and the most difficulty they will meet can be concluded into two types, the first when is that there is heavy smoke, hence the point of fire is quite difficulty to find out, and this point is the main point that I want to let The Progress play its role. The second difficulty and which is the difficulty that hardest to pass over, that is the body’s burden is too heavy, because when they need to climb up a tall building, they need to carry all their equipment, and which can be a big load for human body, however it is a pity that according to my design and prediction, The Progress cannot help the firefighters carry all their stuff and The Progress can even not go upstairs. But this aroused my interest indeed, because on people’s daily lives, it is quite difficulty to carry a lot of heavy things, hence if I can make something which helps people carry things, I bet that it will effect the whole world.

OK back to our topic, why I need to set up an ultrasonic range finder in the Progress? Because I also want to add the Raspberry Pie graphic transmission, which can be used together with infrared red sensor, and hence the point of fire can be showed directly, but it need some high technology equipment, hence I decide to add them at last if possible,which can be more efficiency. And in recent stage, the ultrasonic range finder can help the firefighter the probable area of the fire, corporate with thermocouple, we can easily now the temperature in different areas of the site of fire.
