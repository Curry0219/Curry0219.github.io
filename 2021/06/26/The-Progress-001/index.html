The Progress 001

Posted on 2021-06-26

It has been several months since I began to prepare for the CAIE international examination. Hence I come back and upgrade my project as soon as possible.

Well in the past several months, I had a clearer mind for The Progress. At the very beginning, I thought that I can use The Progress to do some exploration in the desert, but then I noticed that there is not much meaning and clear purpose to launch a rover to the desert, it is also not possible to discover mine covered in the deep earth by my rover, the possibility to find the survivors in the desert is also too small. Hence I watched my draft and I have thought for a very long time, however I didn’t come out any valuable thoughts. And later, accidently, during the time I take break, I found the old news in my WeChat moments, which is about the mountain fire in the Australia. I suddenly come up with an idea about if I can let my rover as an assistor of the firemen to help put on the fire. But in order to verify the rover that I imagine is useful and effective, I went to several websites in China, I noticed that the condition in the fire is too equivocal, and the firemen can only see how the things develop when they enter the fire with protect suits. Hence from my own prospective, it is quite necessary for the firemen to get to know the condition in the fire before they enter it. Hence two hours later, after my own consider, I decide to shift The Progress from a desert explorer with no purpose to a firemen’s assistant, prospect the fire condition and report the data and graphs to the firemen in order to let them work with higher efficiency.

The 3D model almost doesn’t change, and I completed it better. The biggest problem is how to keep The Progress with low temperature in order to prevent it from being burnt or the electrical parts are damaged because of too high temperature. Hence firstly, the traditionally 3D print material is unavailable, because they are simple plastic and will melt at the temperature about 200℃, which cannot bear the temperature around 400℃ in the fire. Hence after I do my research and do communication with my tutor in Nanjing University, I knew a material that called ABS-FRO can bear the temperature about 300℃, and outside the core part which made by the ABS resin, I want to add carbon boards outside the hole rover in order to prevent the rover itself touch the fire directly. And with a layer of Nano aerogel felt, whose thermal conductivity is extremely low, with this layer, the rover can not only be prevent from fire, but also stay in a relatively very low temperature. Which can ensure the safety of the rover.

And this is mainly all about how to apply the heat insulation on The Progress, and at last I am going to show you my draft about some improvement, and in the next blog I am also going to introduce my idea about “External circulation water cooling“.
