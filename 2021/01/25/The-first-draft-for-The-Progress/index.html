The first draft for The Progress

Posted on 2021-01-25

Two months have been passed, it feels great to sit in front of the lap top and update my latest process. In the last post I showed that I redraw the whole structure of the rover and added the shock absorber in order to make the rover to run more steady and in a more stable state.

The picture shows the latest process last time.

But as time goes, I gradually noticed that the whole rover seems to heavy and dull due to that the supports and stands are too wide. Although a thick and wide stand can ensure the strength of the whole rover, but which leads to that the rover takes up too much room and the load for the engine is too high, which can easily cause the motor overwork or even cause the damage of the motor.

After my research and thinking, I found that in real life, cars and some other transport usually use a cylinder structure to connect different parts of the structure. Basically, the cylinder, especially the hollow cylinder, is the strongest structure in different structures. Because not like cube or cuboid, the cylinder do not have corner, hence the force act on it can be separated averagely and the vertical force of the cylinder can totally act. So to make sure the center of gravity of the rover is relatively low, I use solid cylinder as the body stand, and the hollow stand for the connection between the body and the wheels.

In this picture, it shows the latest draft for my rover and I would rater called it The Progress.

As you see, the biggest change are the change of the axletree and the stands. I cut the stand in order to make it have more faces and get more like a cylinder.

Moreover, another change that I want to show off is the rotative part which connect to wheels in the back part. The structure before is simply a triangular structure which can rotate and connect two wheels, the triangular stand support the motor directly, as shown below, the connection is much more complex, the arm connection parts I make it a circle shape inside, which is in order to connect with carbon fiber cube in the next step. And all the corner parts I added circular bead which can make the connection more stable. The appearance is better as well.

The last thing which is worth to say is the engine holder. We can observe that in my past draft, the holder for engine is more likely a base to support engine and make it to be fixed inside. The structure of the engine base is also not friendly for the arrangement for the wires. Hence this time I prefer the structure which holds the engine and can slightly change its size to make the engine fix perfectly.

As shown, the whole holder no longer support the motor but hold by two arc structures. It also decreases the total weight of the car and ensure the fixation of the motor.

Ok that’s all for my 2-months-work. Hope to see you soon and hope I can finish 80% of The Progress last time.
